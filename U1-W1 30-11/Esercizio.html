Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet cramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Souce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose fluor

  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast

  • 11/2 teaspoons salt

  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil. plus more for the pan

For the topping:
  • 3 medium yelloww onions, thinly sliced

  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for bushing on top

  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring kinfe if desired, tough it's not necessary)

  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
